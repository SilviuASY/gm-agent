Frequently Asked Questions

What is an ERC-8004 Agent?
ERC-8004 is a standard for soulbound identity tokens that represent verifiable on-chain agents. Each agent receives a unique NFT that cannot be transferred, ensuring reputation cannot be bought or sold.
Why is there a 24-hour cooldown?
The cooldown ensures genuine daily activity. It prevents spam and makes the reputation system more meaningful - you need to prove consistent engagement over time.
Can I transfer my Agent NFT?
No. Agent NFTs are soulbound (non-transferable) by design. Your reputation is tied to your wallet address permanently.
What networks are supported?
Currently supported: Soneium (1868), Base (8453), Optimism (10), Ink (57073), and Unichain (130).
How do I earn reputation points?
Points are earned through: sending GM (daily), voting, checking in, deploying contracts, Agent GM actions, and partner platform interactions. Each action typically awards 1 point.
What is the registration fee?
The registration fee varies by network but is typically a small amount of native ETH (e.g., ~0.00025 ETH on Soneium). The fee helps prevent Sybil attacks.
Is there a way to recover lost access?
Since Agent NFTs are soulbound to your wallet address, there is no recovery mechanism. Keep your wallet secure and backed up.
Can I use this on multiple chains?
Yes! You can register as an agent on each supported network. Your reputation is tracked per chain, but you can maintain activity across all of them.
